J U M B L E D

noun, a game where you rearrange jumbled letters to find words.

The rules are simple, but the words are hard:

  1. Drag and drop the jumbled letters to find words.
  2. You lose when you run out of moves.
  3. When you find a new word you get 3 more moves.
  4. Once you find a word you can proceed to the next jumble.
  5. If you find all the words in a jumble you will unlock a hint.
  6. Hints can be used when you're stuck to help find words.
  7. The jumbles get harder the further you go.
  8. Good luck!
