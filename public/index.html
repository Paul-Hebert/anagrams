J U M B L E D

A game where you rearrange jumbled letters to find words. The rules are simple, but the gameplay gets tricky.

  1. Drag and drop the letters to rearrange them and find words.
  2. You lose when you run out of moves.
  3. When you find a new word you get 3 more moves.
  4. Once you find a word you can proceed to the next jumble.
  5. If you find all the words in a jumble you will unlock a hint you can use to find tricky words.
